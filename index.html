More Than Coffee. A Community Hub Built for Profit.

We launched BrewHub in 2018 with a simple idea: specialty coffee shouldn't require a trust fund. Today, we're the fastest-growing coffee franchise in the Southwest.

🎯

Our Mission

Make third-wave coffee accessible while building profitable neighborhood hubs. We combine premium beans, smart tech, and operational simplicity so owners focus on community, not complexity.

👁️

Our Vision

500 locations by 2028, each owned by local entrepreneurs, not corporations. We're building the Starbucks alternative that actually puts owners first with transparent economics.

⚡

Why We're Different

No $500K buildouts. No 20-page menus. No corporate bureaucracy. Just a proven 850 sq ft model, 12 core drinks executed perfectly, and average 68% gross margins.

Your Path to Ownership

From inquiry to grand opening in 4-6 months. We handle the complexity, you build the business.

1

Discovery & Approval (Week 1-2)

30-min call, review FDD, financial qualification ($85K liquid, $250K net worth). 90% of applicants approved within 48 hours.

2

Site Selection & Lease (Week 3-8)

Our real estate team finds 3-5 approved locations in your territory. We negotiate lease terms and provide demographic analysis.

3

Buildout & Training (Week 9-16)

Turnkey 850 sq ft buildout ($65K-$110K). You complete 2-week intensive training at HQ while we construct your store.

4

Grand Opening & Ongoing Support

We staff your opening week, run local marketing, and provide weekly business coaching. Average first-month revenue: $42K.
